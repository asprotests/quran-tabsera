Privacy Policy

Quran Tabsera Application

Last Updated: April 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Quran Tabsera. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our application.

Quran Tabsera is an educational application designed to help students learn and memorize the Holy Quran under the guidance of teachers. We take our responsibility to protect your data seriously, especially given the trust you place in us for Islamic education.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number (optional), and user role (student or teacher)
  • Learning Progress: Quran reading statistics, pages read, completion status, and learning milestones
  • Audio Recordings: Recitation recordings submitted by students for teacher review and feedback
  • Usage Data: App interaction data, session duration, and feature usage to improve our services
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for technical support

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Providing personalized Quran learning experience
  • Enabling teacher-student communication and feedback
  • Tracking and displaying your learning progress and Hasanat (rewards)
  • Sending important notifications about your learning journey
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Providing technical support when needed

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Secure cloud storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

Data Sharing

Your data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Teachers: Student progress and recordings are shared with assigned teachers for educational purposes
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that help us operate the app (hosting, analytics) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

Account & Data Deletion

You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Upon deletion:

  • Your personal information will be permanently removed from our systems
  • Your learning progress and statistics will be deleted
  • Any audio recordings you submitted will be removed
  • This action cannot be undone
